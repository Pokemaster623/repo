Electrode

Want to know your battery is low without an intrusive alert? Then Electrode is for you!

Electrode adds a "toast" notification for charging and low power similar the ringer and clipboard toasts.

Includes the follow preferences:

  • Low Power Alert
    • Auto-Hide
    • Auto Low Power Mode
  • Charging Alert
    • Auto-Hide
  • Custom Alert Percents

Requires at least iOS 13
